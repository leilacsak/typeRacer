How to Use TypeRacer
  1. Select your preferred difficulty level from the dropdown menu.
  2. Click the Start button to begin the test.
  3. Type the text shown in the box as accurately and quickly as possible in the input area below.
  4. As you type, correct letters will be highlighted in green and incorrect ones in red.
  5. Click Stop to finish the test and see your results, or Retry to start over with a new text.
  6. Your time and words per minute (WPM) will be displayed in the results area.
